Your questions,answered.

Prehome enables you to move into your dream home today as a renter through a financially beneficial rental plan over a tenure of two years and thereafter convert to a fully-eligible homeowner.

The Prehome program offers the benefit of locking in the property's value on day one, allowing the benefits of capital appreciation to flow to you when you are ready to buy the house after the tenure. During the rental period, customers can build up their savings in order to have the ability to buy the home when they are ready at the end of the tenure.

PreHome's rent-to-own program allows customers to rent a ready-to-move-in apartment with the option to purchase it after the rental period is over. The program enables you to chalk out a clear financial path to homeownership from day one of onboarding.
